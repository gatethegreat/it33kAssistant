## What This Is

Annual organization-wide risk assessment covering IT, operational, and compliance risks. Feeds into the board presentation and determines next year's security budget priorities.

## Goals

- [ ] Complete risk inventory across all departments
- [ ] Score and rank risks by likelihood and impact
- [ ] Identify top 10 risks requiring mitigation plans
- [ ] Draft board-ready summary report
- [ ] Present findings to leadership by Q2 end
