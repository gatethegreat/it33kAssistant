# Risk Scoring Criteria

## 5x5 Matrix

### Likelihood

| Score | Label | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Rare | Less than 5% chance in next 12 months |
| 2 | Unlikely | 5-20% chance |
| 3 | Possible | 20-50% chance |
| 4 | Likely | 50-80% chance |
| 5 | Almost Certain | Greater than 80% chance |

### Impact

| Score | Label | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Negligible | Minor inconvenience, no financial impact |
| 2 | Minor | Limited disruption, <$10K impact |
| 3 | Moderate | Noticeable disruption, $10K-$100K impact |
| 4 | Major | Significant disruption, $100K-$1M impact, potential regulatory notice |
| 5 | Critical | Severe disruption, >$1M impact, regulatory action, reputational damage |

### Risk Rating = Likelihood x Impact

| Rating | Range | Action |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Critical | 20-25 | Immediate mitigation plan required |
| High | 12-19 | Mitigation plan within 30 days |
| Medium | 6-11 | Monitor and review quarterly |
| Low | 1-5 | Accept and review annually |
